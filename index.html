Explore the city of light and its cultural wonders, including the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre.

  • Day 2: Visit the Louvre Museum and explore the Montmartre district.
  • Day 3: Day trip to the Palace of Versailles and gardens.

Discover the perfect blend of tradition and modernity in Japan's bustling capital.
